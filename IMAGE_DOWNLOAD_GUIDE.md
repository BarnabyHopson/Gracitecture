## How to Download from Wikimedia:

1. Go to commons.wikimedia.org
2. Search for the building name
3. Look for images that are:
   - Clear, front-facing facade view
   - High resolution (at least 1200px wide)
   - Public domain or CC0 license
4. Click the image
5. Click "More details" 
6. Click "Use this file" → "Download" → Choose "1600px" or "Original"
7. Save to `/Users/barnabyhopson/Desktop/ArchitectApp/public/images/`
8. Rename to match the format: `building-name.jpg`
